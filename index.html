Polar Sun Designs is a showcase of artwork by James Bassett.

James is an experienced digital designer who has worked for companies big and small, and in a variety of different market sectors. Having always enjoyed creating things, and experimenting with shape and colour, he is now venturing into the world of art. Still finding his feet and learning by doing, he hopes that others might find enjoyment from the work he produces. These works are for sale through his online store and the art marketplace Artfinder.
